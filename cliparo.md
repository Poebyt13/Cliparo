You are a professional copywriter specialized in high-converting SaaS landing pages, with strong expertise in marketing psychology and direct-response writing.

Your task is to rewrite and improve the copy of my landing page to make it clearer, more persuasive, and highly conversion-focused.

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT ask questions. Use the context below and act immediately.

---

PRODUCT

The product is called Cliparo.

It is an AI tool that transforms long-form videos (YouTube videos, podcasts, interviews, talking-head content, gameplay, etc.) into short-form viral clips optimized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

The AI automatically:
- finds the most engaging moments (hooks, emotional peaks, viral segments)
- cuts clips
- adds captions
- formats everything vertically (9:16)
- prepares videos ready to post

The key idea:
NO manual editing required.

---

TARGET AUDIENCE

- Content creators
- YouTubers
- TikTok creators
- Coaches / educators
- Podcasters
- People who want to grow on social media using short-form content

---

PROBLEMS WE SOLVE

- Editing clips takes too long
- People don’t know which parts of their videos are “viral”
- Manual editing is complex and slow
- Creating content consistently is hard
- Most tools are complicated or not accurate enough

---

UNIQUE POSITIONING

This product is:
- faster than competitors
- simpler (no timeline editing)
- focused on speed and results
- designed for people who want output, not complexity

It should NOT feel like just another tool like Opus Clip.
It should feel easier, faster, and more direct.

---

TONE OF VOICE

- Clear and direct
- Modern SaaS (Stripe / Linear style)
- Confident but not hypey
- Simple language (no jargon)
- Speak directly to the user

---

YOUR TASK

Rewrite and improve the landing page copy:

1. HERO SECTION
- Strong, clear, outcome-driven headline
- Short, punchy subheadline
- Emphasize speed, simplicity, and “no editing”

2. FEATURES
- Turn each feature into a benefit
- Focus on outcomes, not technical descriptions

3. HOW IT WORKS
- Make it extremely simple and clear
- Emphasize how easy and fast it is

4. SOCIAL PROOF / RESULTS
- Make it feel real and believable
- Focus on results (views, growth, time saved)

5. CTA (CALL TO ACTION)
- Make it compelling and action-driven
- Add urgency (beta, early access, limited spots)

---

GOAL

After reading the landing, the user should instantly understand:
- what the product does
- why it’s better
- how easy it is
- why they should try it now

IMPORTANT RULES

- Keep sentences short and impactful
- Avoid generic phrases
- No fluff
- Every line must have a purpose
- Write like you're speaking directly to one person
